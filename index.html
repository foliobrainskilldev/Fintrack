Got questions?

Is FinTrack safe to connect to my bank?

Yes. We use industry-leading providers to securely connect to your bank accounts with bank-level 256-bit encryption. We never store or even see your banking credentials. You have read-only access to your data.

Is the web dashboard free?

The mobile app is 100% free for everyday tracking. The advanced web dashboard and pro analytics require a FinTrack Pro subscription, which costs $4.99/month after a 14-day free trial.

Does it work outside the US?

Yes! We support over 10,000 banks across North America, Europe, and parts of Latin America and Asia. The app automatically handles multiple currencies seamlessly.
